Welcome To RNS Logistics

RNS Logistics Established in 2010, RNS Logistics Limited (RNS) is a private limited company in Bangladesh providing comprehensive freight forwarding and logistics management services to national and international clientele. It's progress is directed by a team of management having long years of background in shipping lines, airlines, GSA's and forwarding industry. Trained and experienced personnel at all levels of the company supports management to provide services required by customers. We believe that freight forwarding and logistics management is an important component of supply chain management that is vital for a faster and flexible distribution system. This requires freight forwarders to have the capability to provide innovative solutions. At RNS Logistics limited we have the expertise to provide a complete, client oriented and consistent freight forwarding and logistics service. Our mission is to become a market leader in NVOCC, shipping, aviation, freight and logistics by providing innovative solutions to our customers. Our philosophy is to practice quality system and value based policies in every aspects of our activity to meet the expectations of our customers, our industry, our partners and our employees.
