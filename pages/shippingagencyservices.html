Shipping Agency Services

Since the core activities, during the past 25 years of the Board of Directors, were based on Shipping & Freight Forwarding, prior establishing RNS, we are more than capable of handling any and all of your vessels’ task, in a timely, cost effectively and efficient manner. Our team consists of highly experienced professionals, experienced of handling Break Bulk, Container Vessels, Oil Tankers & Car Carriers.

Based on your vessels’ requirements, we can arrange to supply Fuel, Fresh Water, Food Items etc., within the shortest period of time, at a very competitive rate, without compromising the quality of the supplies.

It addition to the above, we can arrange local Immigration & Port formalities for repatriating crew’s and also the joining crew’s. We would be delighted to respond to any enquiry in this regard.
