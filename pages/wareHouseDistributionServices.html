Warehouse & Distribution Services

In Bangladesh, Freight Forwarders are prohibited from establishing their own Bonded Warehouse. However, all the Off-dock’s have their own Bonded Warehouse inside its bonded premises. Depending on the volume of cargo, a Freight Forwarder handles, the Off-dock’s allocate a designated space, inside which, the Freight Forwarder have to carry out its day-to-day activities. It may be mentioned here that, RNS Logistics Ltd., has only obtained space at Off-dock’s that, had been certified as C-TPAT compliant.

Besides this, special attention is given to detect any suspicious package, which is instantly verified in presence of a Custom Officer, and as well as, the Shipper and our representative. Incoming export cargoes are unloaded from trucks and segregated and stored, as per instruction of Consignee or as per Invoice / Packing List. Upon completion of Custom Inspection and export documentation formalities, RNS will undertake a “Seven Point Check List” of each container prior stuffing, in order to ensure sound & sea worthy condition of the equipment. RNS continues to provide “Zero Defect & Quality Services” at all times and every time. RNS’s cost effective, warehouse, distribution, pick & pack, palletizing, consolidation and de-consolidation services, are compared to none.
