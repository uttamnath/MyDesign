Sea-Air/Air-Sea Services

This is not only a cost effective, but time saving solution as well, for our customers, in order to avoid shipments facing cancellation due to production delays or missing ocean vessel connections etc.. Using this combination of services, suppliers can still ensure arrival of shipment at destination, at a cost, which is a bit higher than ocean freight at shorter transit time and thus, saving the expenses of airfreight cost from origin to destination. Through our extensive network of partners in Hong Kong, Singapore, Colombo, Kuala Lumpur and Dubai, we are providing this incredibly time & currency saving solution to our valuable clients.
