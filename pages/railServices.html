Rail Services

Dhaka being the capital city of Bangladesh, 80% of all major industries /factories are situated in Dhaka. Chittagong at a distance of 276 Kim is the main seaport connected by road & rail from Dhaka & ICD Dhaka. Sometimes export consignments need to avail first available vessel and/or a shipment may require loading at factory premises at Dhaka, in which case, movement from ICD Dhaka by rail to Chittagong is the best option. Depending on the Gross Weight of a laden container, our teams of experts liaise with Railway Officials to allocate designated wagons (with highest payload capacity) to load the containers on priority basis.

Our excellent affiliation with the Bangladesh Railway and Chittagong Port Authority officials, gives us the extra edge in transporting our boxes on priority basis at all times. It may be mentioned here that, availability of empty wagons is inadequate, compared to the number of containers waiting to be loaded on wagons, for onward transportation to & from Dhaka.
